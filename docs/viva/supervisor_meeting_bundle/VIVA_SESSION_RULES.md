# Viva / defence session rules (15 minutes)

Read this **before** you open **`OPEN_FILES_IN_ORDER.md`**. These are the **official** session rules for your meeting.

---

## Logistics

- **Total time:** **15 minutes**. Plan each block. Do not drift.
- **No PowerPoint.** You present on **screen share** only (code, outputs, thesis as needed).
- **Recording:** Sessions are **recorded** under **university policy**. Speak clearly and stay professional.
- **Camera:** You must **turn your camera on**.
- **Start:** After recording starts, say your **full name** and your **Banner ID** (**B01821011**) first.

---

## First 5 minutes (problem and what you did)

You have about **5 minutes** for this block only.

- Say **what the problem is** (IoT flow data, SOC triage, need for explainable detection, federated option, CPU edge story), in **short** lines.
- Say **what you did** to solve it (CICIoT2023, graphs, GAT+GRU, baselines, FedAvg, IG + alerts, metrics).
- **Do not** go into small implementation details here. Save file-level detail for screen share.

**Goal:** They understand the **aim** and **your** pipeline before you show files.

---

## Screen share (existing work, not live full runs)

- **Share your screen** and **show** your work.
- You are **not** expected to **run** every script **one by one** during the slot.
- **Present and explain** using **existing code** and **existing outputs** (for example **`results/metrics/`**, figures, **`example_alerts.json`**).
- Use **`OPEN_FILES_IN_ORDER.md`** as your **tab order** if you open files from this bundle, or the same paths in the **main repo**.

**Tip:** Pre-open a few tabs (`config/experiment.yaml`, one model file, `results_table.csv`, one figure) so you do not waste time searching.

---

## Questions

- **Supervisor** and **moderator** will ask questions.
- Answer **carefully** and **honestly**. If you are unsure, say so and point to what you **did** implement or what is a **limit** in Chapter 9.

---

## Suggested time split (guide only)

| Block | About | Minutes |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Name, ID, hello | Recording start | ~0.5 |
| Problem + solution (high level) | No deep code | ~4.5 |
| Screen share | Code + outputs already there | ~7 |
| Buffer + first questions | If time allows | ~3 |

Adjust if they interrupt with questions earlier.

---

## After the 15 minutes

Question time may continue **after** the formal window depending on programme rules. Still answer **honestly**.
